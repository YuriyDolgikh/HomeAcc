Setting up the 'Home Accounting' application.

In the 'Accounts' section You have the possibility to create and manage a list of accounts. For each account, you can specify the name (displayed in payments), description, type and currency.

For your convenience, the 'Payment Categories' section has a pre-set list of categories that you can assign to your payments. You can delete any of them or add your own. For each category, you can specify a name (displayed in payments) and a description.

It is also possible to change your personal data.
